Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to AsiaBillions, a skill-based number selection gaming platform. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our mobile application and services.

By using AsiaBillions, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Important Notice: AsiaBillions is a skill-based gaming platform where users pay entry fees to participate in number selection games. This is not gambling or betting, as users pay a fixed entry fee to play games with predetermined reward structures.

  • Payment information (for wallet top-ups via supported payment methods)
  • Bank account details (for reward distribution, when applicable)

2.2 Game and Usage Data

  • Game selections and number choices
  • Wallet balance and transaction history

  • Win/loss records and reward history

2.3 Technical Information

  • Device information and operating system
  • IP address and location data

  • Login timestamps and session data

  • Account Management: Creating and maintaining your user account

  • Payment Processing: Handling wallet top-ups through supported payment methods
  • Reward Distribution: Notifying winners and processing reward payments to provided bank accounts
  • Customer Support: Responding to inquiries and providing technical assistance
  • Platform Security: Preventing fraud, ensuring fair play, and maintaining platform integrity
  • Communication: Sending important notifications about games, draws, and account updates

4. Wallet and Payment Information

4.1 Wallet System

Your wallet balance is used exclusively for game entry fees. Once funds are added to your wallet through our supported payment methods, they cannot be withdrawn and are dedicated to game participation only.

4.2 Payment Processing

We use secure, third-party payment processors for wallet top-ups. We do not store complete payment card information on our servers. Payment data is encrypted and processed according to industry standards.

4.3 Reward Distribution

Game rewards are distributed separately from wallet funds. Winners must provide bank account details for reward transfers, which are processed within 3 business days of contact verification.
